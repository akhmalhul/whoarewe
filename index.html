Who are we?

We Dharmatek are "SINGLE POINT SOLUTION WITH PREMIUM MACHINERY FOR TEXTILE". 

 

DHARMATEK BERKAT UTAMA (DHARMATEK) is one of the most careers agency about textile machinery, accessories and spare parts in Indonesia, who provides customers satisfaction by a wide range of products and services to answer their needs to find the right machines from Europe and Asia. We offer technical solutions and products for Pretreatment, Preparation, Finishing – Processing, Dosing & Dyeing System for Laboratory and Bulk Production, Inspection & Packaging Department Designs and Textures surfaces as added value of fabric.

 

DHARMATEK also offer all kinds of technology upgrade, recondition and capacity extensions as in hardware & software for existing machinery for a perfect production flexibility with sustainability, user friendly and less maintenance.

 

DHARMATEK represents textile machinery, spare parts, related equipment and accessories from our reliable suppliers in Europe and Asia.


We always endeavor to become reliable partner with all of our principles and strive to sustain a relationship that is proactive and long lasting.
